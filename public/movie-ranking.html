The Best Movies According to Aryan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Death Note

This is my favourite anime.I love its storyline.It shows how an intelligent high school student goes on a secret crusade to eliminate criminals from the world after discovering a notebook capable of killing anyone whose name is written into it.

Interstellar

Really cool sci-fci movie.It depicts how Earth is no longer inhabitable and humans are in search of a new planet habitable for humans.

Drishyam

Super beautiful film loaded with drama, crime and mystery.It shows how desperate measures are taken by a man who tries to save his family from the dark side of the law, after they commit an unexpected crime.
